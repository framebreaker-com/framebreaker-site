What Is Via Negativa?

The via negativa (Latin: "negative way") is a mystical approach that describes ultimate reality by saying what it is not, rather than what it is.

It acknowledges that language and concepts are always too limited to capture the ineffable.

The Two Ways

Risk: reduces infinite to conceptRisk: too abstract
Both are needed. Via negativa goes deeper.
