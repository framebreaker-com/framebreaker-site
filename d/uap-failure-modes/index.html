Measurement Limitations

Coherence Measurement

Problem: Œ£ is not directly measurable. Current proxies:
  • Paraphrase accuracy
  • Question quality
  • Response appropriateness
  • Long-term stability
Limitation: Proxies can be gamed.

Forcing Detection

Problem: Forcing is not always obvious. Current detection:
  • Explicit threats: clear
  • Time pressure: detectable
  • Subtle framing: hard
  • Structural coercion: context-dependent
Limitation: Sophisticated forcing is hard to detect.

Consent Verification

Problem: "Yes" doesn't always mean yes. Current verification:
  • Check consistency
  • Ask follow-up questions
  • Observe subsequent behavior
  • Allow time for change
Limitation: Simulated consent can be sophisticated.

System-Level Failures

Scalability

Problem: UAP is designed for dyadic interaction. How does it scale? Known issues:
  • Multi-party consent is complex
  • Coherence measurement across groups is hard
  • Recovery in organizations is political

Speed

Problem: UAP requires verification, which takes time. Trade-off:
  • Fast interaction may skip checks
  • Critical interactions need full protocol
  • Context determines appropriate level

Adversarial Environments

Problem: What if the other party is deliberately adversarial? Limitation:
  • UAP cannot force participation
  • UAP cannot prevent all manipulation
  • UAP can only keep your side clean

Honest Assessment

What UAP Can Do

  • Provide structure for stable interaction
  • Detect obvious forcing
  • Enable recovery from drift
  • Scale from conversation to protocol design

What UAP Cannot Do

  • Prevent all manipulation
  • Work without minimal good faith
  • Guarantee correct understanding
  • Force adversaries to cooperate
