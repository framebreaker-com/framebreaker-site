The Core Claim

Ethical action = interaction under UAP conditions.

Not because UAP defines good.

But because UAP ensures:

  • No one is forced
  • Everyone is understood
  • Mistakes can be repaired

The Emergence Claim

Hypothesis: Many ethical problems arise not from "bad intentions" but from structural forcing in interaction.

UAP eliminates that structure ‚Äî and lets ethics emerge from mutual understanding. No new morality needed. Just a better grammar.
