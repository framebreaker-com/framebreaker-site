What Tartaria Actually Was

"Tartary" or "Tartaria" was a European term (16th-19th century) for a large, vaguely known region in Central Asia and Siberia ‚Äî roughly from the Caspian Sea to the Pacific, including Mongolia, parts of China and Russia.

On old maps (1500-1800) you see "Great Tartary", "Independent Tartary", "Chinese Tartary".

It was not a unified empire.

It was a blank spot on the map: unknown territory inhabited by nomadic peoples (Tatars, Mongols).

Why Did It Disappear?

  • Russia colonized Siberia
  • China expanded
  • Better cartography ‚Üí specific names (Siberia, Mongolia)
No "erasure" ‚Äî just becoming more precise.
