UAP-Compliant Contract Process

Phase 1: Meta-Consent (C‚ÇÄ)

"Do you want to enter a contractual relationship about X?"

Not assumed. Explicitly asked.

Phase 2: Intent Verification (C‚ÇÅ + Œ£)

For each major term:

  • Party A states understanding
  • Party B confirms or corrects
  • Iterate until match
"I understand that delivery happens within 30 days. Correct?"

Phase 3: Lock (L)

Only after verified mutual understanding:

  • Both parties confirm identical interpretation
  • This is the actual agreement

Phase 4: Recovery Protocol (R)

Built into the contract:

  • Dispute = return to last valid lock
  • Re-derive intentions from that point
  • No punishment for seeking clarification

Implications for Legal Practice

For Contract Drafting

  • Include explicit paraphrase sections
  • Require confirmation of understanding per section
  • Build in recovery protocol
  • Document the consent process, not just the terms

For Dispute Resolution

  • Start with: "What was the last point of mutual understanding?"
  • Work forward from there
  • Focus on intent verification, not blame
  • Recovery before litigation

For Terms of Service

  • Stop pretending "by using you agree" is consent
  • Offer genuine opt-out
  • Verify understanding of key terms
  • Make rejection costless
