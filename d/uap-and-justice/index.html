The Radical Claim

Hypothesis: Most injustice arises not from "bad actors" but from structural forcing in the justice system itself.

  • Plea deals = forcing
  • Power asymmetry = forcing
  • Punishment without understanding = forcing
UAP eliminates that structure ‚Äî and lets justice emerge from mutual understanding.

Practical Implications

For Legal Systems

  • Consent must be genuinely voluntary
  • Understanding must be verified before judgment
  • Repair should be default, punishment exception
  • Power asymmetries should be actively countered

For Conflict Resolution

  • Start with C‚ÇÄ: "Do you want to resolve this?"
  • Continue with C‚ÇÅ: "Do I understand your position?"
  • Aim for L: mutual agreement
  • Fall back to R: repair upon failure

For Everyday Justice

  • Don't judge without understanding
  • Don't punish without consent to process
  • Don't force resolution
  • Always offer repair
