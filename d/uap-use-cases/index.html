7. DeFi / Smart Contracts

Situation

Two parties interact via a smart contract.

Without UAP

  • Flash loan attacks
  • Unilateral liquidations
  • Cascades
  • Loss of trust in system

With UAP

Contract design:
- No action that reduces counterparty options without 
  explicit consent-gate (multi-sig or timelock)
- Recovery window for disputes
- Forcing detection (rate limits, circuit breakers)

Result

  • More robust protocols
  • Fewer exploits
  • Higher trust
