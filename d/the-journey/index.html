The Journey

From Everything to Essence

What You're Looking At

This repository contains 115 files and 300KB of documentation.

It took months to create.

Not by adding.

By removing.

The Process

Phase 1: Building Without Design

We started by making things:

  • Books
  • Apps
  • Protocols
  • Experiments
No grand theory. Just: "What works?"

Phase 2: Extensive Documentation

Then we tried to capture everything:

  • Formal specifications
  • Detailed descriptions
  • Edge cases
  • Elaborate PID (Project Initiation Document)
Hundreds of pages. Every detail.

Phase 3: Reduction (INIT0)

Then we asked:

  • What is essential?
  • What can be removed?
  • What remains?
And we kept removing until only the core was left.

Phase 4: One Question

Everything reduced to four words:

"Do I understand you correctly?"

What Is NOT In This Repository

  • The original books and apps
  • The elaborate PID documents
  • The intermediate versions
  • The dead ends
  • The hundreds of pages that didn't survive reduction
This is not a bug. This is the method.

The Irony

We spent:

  • Months of work
  • Hundreds of pages written
  • Four AIs collaborating
  • Countless iterations
To discover that the whole thing fits in four words.

INIT0

This is what INIT0 means:

Start from zero. Remove everything that isn't essential. What remains is the protocol.

The journey is not the product.

The kernel is the product.
