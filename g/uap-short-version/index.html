How Forcing Works

Forcing = reducing someone's response options without their consent.

Examples:

  • Assuming you know what someone means (without checking)
  • Making decisions that affect others (without asking)
  • Continuing a conversation someone doesn't want
  • Using time pressure to limit choices
Anti-forcing = always leaving space for "no".

What Makes This Different

Most protocols focus on what to communicate.
