The Paradox of Practice

Trying too hard is forcing. Forcing reduces presence. Therefore: light practice.

  • Not rigid discipline
  • Not constant monitoring
  • Just: notice, question, return
Like breathing:
  • You don't force each breath
  • You just notice when it stops
  • And let it resume
