Capitalism vs. UAP

Capitalism Works When

  • Exchange is voluntary
  • Competition prevents forcing
  • Property rights are clear
  • Contracts are enforceable

Capitalism Fails When

  • Wealth concentration enables forcing
  • "Take it or leave it" becomes norm
  • Essential goods have no alternative
  • Power asymmetry > consent capacity
Capitalism is UAP-compatible only under anti-monopoly conditions.

Socialism vs. UAP

Socialism Works When

  • Collective decisions are consensual
  • Central planning reflects actual preferences
  • Distribution is agreed, not forced

Socialism Fails When

  • Central authority imposes preferences
  • Dissent is suppressed
  • Exit is not possible
Socialism is UAP-compatible only under genuine consent conditions.

The Deeper Pattern

Neither capitalism nor socialism is inherently UAP-compliant.

Both can implement UAP:

  • Capitalism: voluntary exchange + anti-monopoly
  • Socialism: genuine consent + exit rights
Both can violate UAP:
  • Capitalism: monopoly forcing
  • Socialism: central forcing
The system matters less than the structure.
