The Formula

$$\text{Mastery} = \lim_{\text{drift} \to \text{recovery}} (t_{\text{gap}} \to 0, F_{\text{return}} \to 0)$$

Mastery is:
  • Quick recognition of drift
  • Soft, fast return
  • No forcing in either direction

The Paradox

The goal is not to never drift.

The goal is to become so good at recovery that drift doesn't matter.

Like breathing:

  • Inhale happens
  • Exhale happens
  • Neither is a problem
Like walking:
  • Off balance happens
  • Recovery happens
  • Neither is a problem
