Why We Get Distracted

Biological Reasons

  • Novelty-seeking (survival advantage)
  • Threat detection (amygdala always scanning)
  • Social monitoring (tribal survival)

Design Reasons

  • Apps engineered for engagement
  • Variable reward schedules
  • Social validation metrics

Psychological Reasons

  • Avoidance of discomfort
  • Escape from boredom
  • Relief from uncertainty
None of these are moral failures.
