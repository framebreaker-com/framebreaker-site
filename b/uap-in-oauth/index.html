Layered Consent in Detail

Layer 1: Meta-Consent (C‚ÇÄ)

The consent screen asks: "Do you want to allow this application to access your account?"

This is not about specific permissions yet ‚Äî it's about the interaction itself.

Layer 2: Content-Consent (C‚ÇÅ)

Scopes specify exactly what is being consented to:

- email (read your email address)
- profile (read your basic profile)
- calendar.readonly (read your calendar)
- calendar.write (modify your calendar)

Anti-Forcing in OAuth

Scope Limitation

An app cannot request more access than it was granted:

Requested: email, profile
Granted: email, profile
Attempted: calendar.write
Result: 403 Forbidden (F‚Åª violation blocked)

No Implicit Consent

OAuth explicitly rejects "bundled consent":

  • Each permission is listed separately
  • User must actively approve
  • Defaults are not allowed
