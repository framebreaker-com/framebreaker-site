Legal Considerations

Disclaimers, Warranties, and Anti-Misuse

General Disclaimer

UAP is presented as an operational interaction protocol and research artifact.

It is not:

  • Medical advice
  • Psychological treatment
  • Legal counsel
  • Security guarantee
  • Compliance certification
No outcomes are guaranteed. Effects depend on context, implementation, and participant consent.

No Warranty

This work is provided "as is", without warranties of any kind, express or implied.

Use at your own risk.

The authors and contributors:

  • Make no claims about fitness for any particular purpose
  • Accept no liability for outcomes of use
  • Do not guarantee any results

Anti-Misuse Clause

Any deployment that:
  • Coerces participants
  • Removes opt-out capability
  • Uses the protocol to compel assent
  • Weaponizes verification against individuals
  • Deploys without informed consent
...is non-conformant with UAP by definition.

UAP cannot be used against its own principles. Forcing someone to follow UAP is itself a UAP violation.

Specific Domain Disclaimers

Medical / Psychological

Claims about cognition, therapy, or brain function are hypotheses, not treatment recommendations.

If you are experiencing mental health issues, please consult a qualified professional.

Security

Claims about security benefits are design patterns, not guarantees.

No system is immune to attack. Threat modeling and professional security review remain necessary.

AI Alignment

UAP is a governance grammar, not an alignment proof.

It specifies interaction structure, not AI safety guarantees.

Organizational

Claims about team effectiveness are hypotheses based on limited observation.

Organizational outcomes depend on many factors beyond protocol choice.
