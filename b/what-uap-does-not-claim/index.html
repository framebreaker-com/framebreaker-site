What UAP Does NOT Claim

Explicit Non-Claims and Limitations

Purpose of This Document

To be clear about boundaries.

UAP is easy to misread as overclaiming. This document exists to prevent that.

UAP Is NOT

Not a Theory of Everything

UAP describes interaction structure. It does not explain:

  • Physics
  • Consciousness
  • The origin of the universe
  • Why things exist
It's a grammar, not an ontology.

Not a Cure

UAP is not therapy. It does not:

  • Heal trauma
  • Fix mental illness
  • Replace professional treatment
  • Guarantee wellbeing
It may support therapeutic processes, but it is not treatment.

Not a Guarantee

UAP does not guarantee:

  • Good outcomes
  • Alignment
  • Safety
  • Success
  • Understanding
It provides structure, not certainty.

Not Complete

UAP does not claim to be:

  • The only valid framework
  • The final word
  • Unfalsifiable
  • Beyond criticism
It's a working hypothesis, not revealed truth.

Not Original

Many elements of UAP exist in:

  • Clean Language
  • Nonviolent Communication
  • Rogerian therapy
  • Socratic method
  • Various wisdom traditions
UAP formalizes patterns that humans have discovered repeatedly. It doesn't invent them.

The "Perfect" Question

What we mean by "internally consistent"

  • No contradictions within the specification
  • Self-applicable (UAP works on UAP)
  • Minimal (nothing unnecessary)

What we do NOT mean

  • Infallible
  • Complete
  • Beyond improvement
  • The only way

Falsifiability

UAP is designed to be falsifiable.

If you can show: 1. A stable system that requires forcing 2. Coherence that increases under coercion 3. Recovery that works better through escalation 4. Consent that blocks rather than enables

...then UAP is wrong.

Please try.

The Arrogance Problem

We're aware that presenting a framework that:

  • Claims to work across domains
  • Has formal structure
  • Uses confident language
...can sound arrogant.

Our response
